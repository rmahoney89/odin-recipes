This is the best chocolate chip cookies recipe ever! No funny ingredients, no chilling time, etc. Just a simple, straightforward, amazingly delicious, doughy yet still fully cooked, chocolate chip cookie that turns out perfectly every single time!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugare
  • 1 cup light bown sugar (packed)
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chunks

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. In a separate bowl, ombine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and sea salt in a bowl and mix.
  3. Cream together the butter and sugar
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract to creamed butter/sugar mixture
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients
  6. Add chocolate chunks and mix well
  7. Roll spoonfuls of dough into balls and place evenly on a cookie sheet
  8. Bake for approximately 9-10 minutes, until golden on the edges
  9. Let cookies sit for 10-15 minutes before enjoying!
